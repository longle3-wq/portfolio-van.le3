The Challenge

The fundamental challenge of the 1Hub landing page was bridging two vastly different worlds: complex Web3 technology (multi-chain wallets, decentralized digital IDs) and everyday physical utilities (smart lockers, e-bike rentals, and campus events). Because 1Hub is a "Super App" that does almost everything, the risk of cognitive overload was incredibly high. The landing page needed to avoid intimidating mainstream users with heavy blockchain jargon while still proving its technical capabilities to tech-savvy early adopters.

Hub

The Solution

To solve this, the landing page was designed as a narrative-driven, modular experience that translates technical features into tangible human benefits. Instead of listing features, the design leverages progressive disclosure, leading with a single, unifying value proposition ("One Digital Identity for Everything") in the hero section. By grounding the complex Web3 infrastructure in relatable, real-world daily tasks, the page builds trust, ensures scanability, and drives app downloads without overwhelming the user.
